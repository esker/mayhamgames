Adventurers, Join us in the murky depths of the ever-deeper, ever-darker, CIMMERIAN FOREST! Within these woods are creatures of mystery, cunning, and immense danger. We are looking to find the scrappiest, ragtag team of trappers to capture these creatures to use for display for all to see.
In this 2-5 player fully cooperative game, you must work together to capture them alive -- if you can stomach the grotesque (and comical) beings that lie within!
